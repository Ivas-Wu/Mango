How to Play

Classic Mode:

Win the game by marking any row, colunm or diagonal on your game board!

Completion Mode:

Win the game by marking the entire board!

Classic Mode:

Click the "Next turn" button to get more numbers. Try and win in the least number of turns!

Timer Mode:

Every x seconds a new set of numbers will be presented. Try and win as fast as possible!

Addition and Multiplication:

Select all the numbers you want first, and then hit the operation button to apply it to all of them!

Subtraction and Division:

Select 2 numbers. The second number will be subtracted/divided from the first number. If the result must be a positive integer.

Clear:

Will clear all marked, selected and held numbers, bingo board squares and phantom numbers. Any completed bingo board squares that are selected and its components will also be cleared

Clear All:

Will clear all marked, selected, held and completed squares of all types.
